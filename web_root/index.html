03 · Ask before you tow it

Honest answers, no fine print.

stuff people actually call to ask
Do I need an appointment?

Calling first helps — we can usually save you a slot, and on busy weeks it's the difference between same-day and next-week. Walk-ins are welcome too. Worst case we'll tell you when to come back.

Will you give me a price before you start?

Always. We diagnose first, then call you with what we found and what it'll cost. Nothing gets touched until you say go. If we find more once we're in there, we stop and call again.

Do you work on classic cars and weird stuff?

Yes. Pre-war American iron, Tri-Five Chevys, vintage trucks, project cars, small engines, fleet — if it's got pistons or wires, we want to look at it. That's what the "and…" is for.

What if it's not worth fixing?

We'll tell you. We'd rather lose a job than sell you one that doesn't make sense. That's how we still have customers from ten years ago.

How do I drop it off?

Pull in during business hours and we'll take a look right then. After-hours drop is fine too — call ahead, we'll tell you where to leave the keys.
