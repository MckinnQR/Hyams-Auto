About Hyams Auto

The "and…" matters.

We are a full automotive service specialist garage in Blue Ash, Ohio. The "and…" in our name is for our love of anything with a motor.

What we do here

Hyams Auto is a full automotive service specialist garage. We work on cars and trucks of every make and model — daily drivers, family vehicles, work trucks, and the project car gathering dust in your garage. If it's broke, we can fix it.

"If it's broke, we can fix it." — That's not a slogan we put on a wall. It's how we decide whether to take a job.

Why the "and…"

The dots after our name aren't a typo. They're there because we don't only do cars. The "and…" is for our love of anything with a motor — bikes, small engines, fleet equipment, the odd machine you can't find anyone else willing to look at.

Bring it in. We'll take a look and tell you straight what it needs and what it'll cost.

How we work

We diagnose before we quote, we tell you what we find, and we don't add work that doesn't need doing. If it's not worth fixing, we'll tell you that too. That's how a shop earns repeat customers — and it's how we've built a 100% recommendation rate from the people who've walked through our door.

Who we serve

We're based in Blue Ash and serve drivers across the greater Cincinnati area — Montgomery, Sycamore, Mason, Sharonville, Kenwood, and the surrounding communities. Walk-ins welcome; calls preferred so we can save you a slot.
